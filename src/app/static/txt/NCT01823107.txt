Inclusion Criteria:

1.      Non-smoker
2.      Undergoing unilateral or bilateral, two-stage, tissue expander-assisted breast reconstruction
3.      Life expectancy greater than 18 months
4.      Agreement to return for the trial required follow-up visits

Exclusion Criteria:

1.	Body mass index ³ 35
2.	Prior reconstructive breast surgery, breast augmentation, mastopexy or reduction mammoplasty
3.	History of chronic corticosteroid use
4.	Type I Diabetes
5.	History of radiation therapy to the chest
6.	Treatment of pre-operative induction chemotherapy for breast cancer
7.	Pregnancy
8.	Participating in another investigational drug or device trial that has not completed the follow-up period
