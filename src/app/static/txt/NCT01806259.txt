Inclusion Criteria:

1.      Written informed Consent
2.      age : 18-85 years
3.      weight: 50-100 kg
4.      Neutrophils / Lymphocytes ratio >4 and/or "triple negative" histological status and/or Positive lymph nodes

Exclusion Criteria:

1.	Previous cancer (behalf of basocellular skin cancer and in situ uterine cervix cancer)
2.	Non compliance or refusal of the protocol
3.	Positive Pregnancy test
4.	Childbearing or breastfeeding mothers
5.	Contra-indication for NSAIDs
6.	NSAIDs intake in the 5 days before randomisation
7.	NSAIDs use planned in the 30 days after randomisation
8.	Non curative surgery (T4 or M1 tumor classification )
